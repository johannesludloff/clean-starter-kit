Manage your Lujo subscription and billing settings.

## View your plan

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. See your current plan and usage

## Upgrade to Pro

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. Click **Upgrade**
3. Select your plan
4. Enter payment details
5. Confirm

Your new features are available immediately.

## Change plans

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. Click **Change plan**
3. Select a new plan
4. Confirm

When upgrading, you're charged the prorated difference. When downgrading, credit is applied to your next billing cycle.

## Update payment method

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. Click **Payment method**
3. Enter new card details
4. Save

## View invoices

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. See your billing history
3. Download any invoice as PDF

## Cancel subscription

1. Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.lujo.app/settings/billing)
2. Click **Cancel subscription**
3. Confirm

You'll retain access until the end of your billing period. Your data is preserved if you decide to resubscribe.
